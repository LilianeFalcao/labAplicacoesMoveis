## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: App Communication Features
The app should support global alerts, announcements, and an interactive feed.

#### Scenario: Feed de Fotos
- **WHEN** parents or monitors open the communication tab
- **THEN** they should see the photo feed as in `feed_de_fotos.png`.

#### Scenario: Avisos e Notificações
- **WHEN** a general alert is sent
- **THEN** it should appear in the alert history as in `avisos_e_notificacoes.png` and `comunicados_gerais.png`.
