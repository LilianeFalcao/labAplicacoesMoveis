## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Activity Photo UI
Monitors must have a dedicated interface to take photos of activities.

#### Scenario: Accessing Camera Preview
- **WHEN** the monitor navigates to the Capture Photo screen
- **THEN** the camera preview must be displayed in full screen

#### Scenario: Capturing Photo
- **WHEN** the monitor touches the capture button
- **THEN** the system must capture the photo
- **AND** show a preview of the captured image with "Retake" and "Confirm" options
